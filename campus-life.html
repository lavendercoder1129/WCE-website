Life at WCE

Spanning 90 acres of architectural legacy and natural beauty, WCE Sangli is more than an institute—it's a home for innovation and holistic growth.

Campus & Infrastructure

Walchand College of Engineering boasts a sprawling 90-acre campus that perfectly blends historic 1947 architectural charm with modern, state-of-the-art facilities. Our serene, tree-lined environment provides the ideal backdrop for rigorous academic pursuit and personal discovery.

Student Housing & Lifestyle

Hostels & Mess

With separate accommodation for 1500+ students, our hostels offer a vibrant community life. Each hostel is equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi, modern mess facilities, and common rooms for recreation.

Sustainability

WCE is committed to a 'Green Campus' initiative, featuring solar power grids, rainwater harvesting, and waste management systems integrated into our sprawling landscape.

Dr. Surendra Singh Rathod

Director, WCE Sangli

Welcome to India's premier technological legacy. At WCE, we are committed to nurturing innovation and excellence in the next generation of engineers.

It is a matter of great pride and privilege to lead Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli—an institution that has stood as a beacon of academic discipline and technological innovation since 1947. As we navigate the complexities of the 21st century, our focus remains steadfast on creating a student-centric learning ecosystem.

We believe that education must transcend traditional boundaries. By integrating Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS) with cutting-edge technical education, we aim to produce graduates who are not only technically proficient but also socially responsible and culturally rooted. Our autonomy allows us the flexibility to keep our curriculum aligned with the rapidly evolving global landscape.

Research, innovation, and industry-academia partnerships are the cornerstones of our growth strategy. We invite you to be a part of this journey towards excellence, where tradition meets modern innovation.

🚀 Innovation First

Promoting a culture of research and patent-driven development across all engineering departments.

🤝 Industry Linkage

Strengthening partnerships with global giants like Google, Amazon, and Microsoft for placements and research.

🌱 Holistic Growth

Focusing on life skills, ethics, and social responsibility alongside core technical expertise.

🏛️ Heritage & Future

Maintaining our 75-year legacy while implementing NEP 2020 and modern academic standards.

Dr. Surendra Singh Rathod

Director, Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli

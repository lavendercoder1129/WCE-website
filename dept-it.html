Department Overview

Digital Innovation Hub

Established in 2001, the Department of Information Technology at WCE has been at the center of the silicon revolution in Sangli, fostering world-class software engineers for over two decades.

NBA Accredited Excellence

Our programs are recognized for their high academic standards and industry-aligned curriculum, ensuring that students are day-one ready for global tech roles.

Specialized Learning Tracks

We offer specialized honors and minor certifications in Cyber Security, Blockchain Technology, and Cloud Computing.

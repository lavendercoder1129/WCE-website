Campus Culture & Clubs

Where passion meets purpose. Explore 30+ student organizations and a legacy of vibrant celebrations.

The Heart of Walchand Experience

WCE is more than just an institute; it's a thriving ecosystem of creativity, technology, and sportsmanship. Our student-led organizations provide a platform for holistic development, leadership, and lifelong friendships.

💻

Technical Excellence

Nurturing future engineers through coding, robotics, and core branch innovations.

 🎭

Cultural Vibrancy

Celebrating traditions and artistic brilliance through music, drama, and dance.

 🏆

Sports & Fitness

Instilling discipline and team spirit through competitive athletics and games.

 🤝

Social & Personality

Building character and serving society through volunteerism and leadership.
The Big Celebration

Annual Social Gathering

Held every February, the Gathering is a spectacular celebration of talent and spirit. It begins with "Walchand Eve" and features a week of high-octane events that showcase the artistic soul of our college.

Sursangam

Musical and vocal excellence competition.

Navras

High-intensity dramatic performances.

Nrutyansh

Fusion and traditional dance showcase.

Euphoria

A week of traditional and theme days.

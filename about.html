The Walchand Heritage

Founded by the MTE Society and built upon the initiative of the legendary industrialist Seth Walchand Hirachand, Walchand College of Engineering began with just Civil and Mechanical branches during India's transition to independence. Over seven decades later, we have evolved into a Government-Aided Autonomous powerhouse. Situated in the heart of Sangli-Miraj, our campus serves as a beacon of academic discipline and innovative engineering in Western Maharashtra.
